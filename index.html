About me

Hi! my name is Cesario Mendoza. I am from the San Francisco Bay Area ( Go Niners! ). I discovered the internet some years ago when I was dabbling with some Adobe Photoshop at a friends house. My friend let me keep his old copy of Photoshop and once I started to dabble with designs and making graphics of my own, then the rest was history. I bought a few books on HTML and CSS and after I realized what I was able to do on a computer I instantly was hooked. I realized that with CSS and HTML only , I was limited to what I was capable of. Turned out those two languages were just a gateway to more logical programming languages and then here I am.
